How to do a Slicing Design

First is prepare the design in Figma:

-Create your design in Figma by placing all the elements you want to include in your slicing design.

-Make sure that all the layers are properly labeled and organized.

-Set the dimensions and resolution of the design according to your desired output size

How to do a Slicing Design

Export the design from Figma:

-Once you've completed the design, export it by clicking on the "Export" button at the top of the Figma interface.

-Choose the file format (e.g., PNG, JPG) and the resolution you want to use for your output files.
